Bloom Platform

"The Eye in the Sky for Agricultural Stability."

Our platform is a centralized, AI-driven regional monitoring system designed to bridge the gap between raw field data and high-level policy decisions. Unlike traditional surveying methods which take weeks, Kisan Raksha provides real-time visibility into crop distribution across vast geographical sectors.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Satellite Surveillance: Automatically scans and classifies regional imagery to identify crop types (Wheat, Corn, Sugarcane) with 91%+ accuracy.
  • Live Executive Dashboard: Transforms complex pixel data into actionable insights, utilizing dynamic charts to show regional growth percentages instantly.
  • Self-Healing Data Pipeline: Features a robust backend that ensures data integrity and continuous availability for decision-makers, even in low-connectivity environments.

Strategic Market Intelligence

"Preventing Crises Before They Happen."

We move beyond simple observation to provide predictive market intelligence. By analyzing the density of specific crops in a region, our system helps government bodies and agricultural boards maintain market balance.

Solutions:

  • Market Saturation Alerts: Identifies if a region is over-producing a specific crop (e.g., Sugarcane), allowing officials to issue advisories preventing price crashes due to oversupply.
  • Food Security Monitoring: Detects low-growth zones for essential staples like Wheat, triggering early warnings for potential shortages.
  • Resource Allocation: Helps direct water, fertilizer, and subsidies to the sectors that actually contain the targeted crops, eliminating waste.

Technology Stack

"State-of-the-Art Deep Learning Architecture."

Kisan Raksha is built on a modern, cloud-native stack designed for speed, accuracy, and scalability.

Technical Components:

  • Deep Learning Core: Powered by MobileNetV2 with Transfer Learning, optimized for high-speed inference on satellite imagery while maintaining a low computational footprint.
  • FastAPI Backend: A high-performance, asynchronous Python server that handles data aggregation and AI prediction requests in milliseconds.
  • Smart Data Simulation: Utilizes a "Lazy Loading" architecture to manage large datasets efficiently, ensuring instant dashboard startup and zero latency.
  • Cloud Deployment: Hosted on Render, utilizing containerized environments to ensure the system is accessible from any government office, anywhere.
